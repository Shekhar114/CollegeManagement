THE LIBRARY

Fully computerized library has all the necessary and well stocked study material. It is operational for more than 12-16 hours a day. The library has numerous titles, all of which are issued to students on a weekly basis. Apart from all these books, the library also subscribes to a number of journals, magazines and newspaper related to each course.

There is a spacious reading room for the benefit of the students. The students are required to use the library regularly as a part of their curriculum programme. AGI offers a learning environment as diverse and as unique as the interest of the students.
 

INTERNET FACILITY

The computer lab is equipped with state of the art hardware and software and is available for students as per schedule. The center consists of a number of computers - the latest technology. The center has a 2Mbps dedicated radio link for 24 hour connectivity. All computers have full back-up with UPS.
 

CANTEEN / MESS

A spacious and hygienic canteen at AGI provides fresh snacks, tea, coffee, juice, cold drinks and basic items of daily need items to students and visitors.

Food preparation under the direct supervision of the mess committee comprising of management, faculty and student representatives. Tasty wholesome and nourishing food is prepared under strict supervision. Special attention is paid to maintain hygienic condition in the mess and it is regularly monitored.
 

AUDITORIUM

A Platform of interface
1. Adhunik Group of Institutions has training grounds for building professionals
2. Adhunik Group of Institutions provides a state-of-the-art auditorium that acts as a common ground for students, faculty and corporate personalities for regular interfaces, conferences and other events.
3. For small scale events, there are Seminar Halls, which are well equipped with advanced presentation tools. All to make them an ideal venue.
4. At the Adhunik Group of Institutions platform, students will come across various global thought leader, academic guests, Gurus and corporate heads who will shere thier corporate experience with them.
 

SYNDICATE ROOMS AND LABS

The syndicate room provide a perfect setting for students to master team skills through exercise like brain storming sessions, group formation, role plays, group discussions etc.
Adhunik Group of Institution has lab that allow the students to experiment and bring into practice what they learnt in theory.
latest technology resources are present in labs to spread awareness and give the best education.
 

HOSTEL

The campus hostels, which are located in calm and serene surroundings, provide a natural atmosphere conducive to study for the students. To provide a homely atmosphere and a suitable ambience for studying, the Group has separate hostels for boys and girls. All these hostels have separate wardens who are devoted to their task and look after the welfare of the students with care and dedication. The hostels are located away from each other with separate mess facilities. The mess facilities cater to the needs of all the students by providing both North and South Indian vegetarian food. Utmost care is taken to maintain hygiene during preparation and serving by a dedicated staff.
 

SPORT AND RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES

"Be a sport" is the philosophy that AGI firmly believes in We encourage our students to study, play and balance theie time between the two.
AGI has created an excellent infrastructure for sporting activities. There is a volleyball and badminton court in the college grounds.
 

MEDICAL FACILITIES

TO ensure student's well being, Adhunik Group of Institutions has personal health check up centres and tie ups with renowned groups of hospitals to take care of any emergency.
At AGI various activities are conducted such as:
  • Regular medical check-ups
  • Vaccination Camps
  • Dental Check-ups
  • Blood Donation capms
  • Pain relieving exercises.
 

TRANSPORT

AGi provides transport facilities for students, faculty and staff from Delhi, Meerut, Modinagar, Ghaziabad, Gurgaon, Dadri, Bulandshahar and other surrounding areas.

AGI is also located on main Delhi-Meerut Road NCR due to which it is very well connected to various places by local transport (Autorickshaws, state owned buses etc.)
